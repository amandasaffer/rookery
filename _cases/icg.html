The Internet Creators Guild is a non-profit organization that exists to further the industry of online content creation. The ICG is supported by dues from members which helps fund case studies, podcasts, and other resources.

Brand & Identity The Rookery worked with the ICG to create an identity that would define the community from day one. We didn't want the logo to look too modern. Instead, we married the idea of internet creation with a throwback to print publications.

We chose Tablet Gothic for the typeface, which is described as "a grotesque sans serif that looks to the future of digital publishing with a clear understanding of its analogue history."

Web Development We put together the ICG website with Laravel, which allowed us to easily integrate with Stripe to accept subscription payments and Socialite to allow members to link their social media accounts to their membership.

Upon completing registration, new members receive onboarding emails with all the details of their membership, are automatically added to the ICG Mailchimp mailing list, and receive an opt-in Slack invite. The ICG gained over 400 paying members within the first month of launch.
