Background

Josh Owens is a developer, entrepreneur, and teacher in Javascript community. He's also the proprietor of Crater.io, a news aggregator for real-time development. Previously an active member of the Rails community, he moved to Meteor and started Meteor Club—a place for people to learn and network with fellow devs. He's since expanded to include other Javascript technologies, and Spacedojo was born.

My work with Josh has been to design and develop fresh but consistent materials as his brand evolves—including design and development for classes, landing pages, podcasts, events, and more.
