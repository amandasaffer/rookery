Josh Owens is a Javascript developer, entrepreneur, and teacher. Previously an active member of the Rails community, he moved to Meteor and started Meteor Club—a place for people to learn and network with fellow devs. He's since expanded to include other Javascript technologies, and Spacedojo was born.

The Rookery has worked with Josh to design and develop fresh but consistent materials as his brand evolves—including design and development for classes, landing pages, podcasts, events, and more.
